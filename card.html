Tarsier

Tarsier's eyes is as big as their brains.

  • Scientific Name:Tarsiidae
  • Length: 9-16 cm
  • Life Span: 2-20 years
  • Region: Southeast Asia

Tarsiers are nocturnal mammals that are the only primate that is entirely carnivorous. They hunt for insects and lizards, but are hunted by snakes and birds. They are the second smallest primates in the world and are endagered species.
